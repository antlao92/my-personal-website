Green Bay Packers

Ever since I could remember, I have loved the Green Bay Packers! I remember playing madden with my childhood friend and wanting to pick the Oakland Raiders. He would always pick them, so I picked the team right before, which was the Green Bay Packers! I was hooked ever since!

I love playing sports and watching sports! I used sports as a way to escape and develop discipline. My parents and teachers remember me as a kid who couldn't keep still. While I have changed quite a bit, I still really enjoy sports and the lessons that can be learned while playing or watching sports.

I have always liked the Houston Rockets as well. I didn't get to spend a lot of time with my dad because he worked a lot. But when the rockets drafted Yao Ming, 7 foot 6 center from China, we bonded over him being in the NBA. I loved the team ever since. Watching the rockets compete with Yao Ming and Tracy McGrady were unforgettable memories that I relish. I remember one game in particular where the T-mac scores 13 points in 33 seconds. I remembered as a kid jumping up and down in disbelief as it was happening on tnt. It was a sight to behold.
